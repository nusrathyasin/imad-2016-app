Do a barrel roll:

Just type Do a barrel roll in Google search and see how your screen rotates. There are lot more funny things to search on Google. Just continue reading to find out more.

Zerg Rush:

Just type Zerg Rush in Google to play another fun game. You can also share the score of this game.

Use Double Quotes for exact match results:

Using quotes in the search query will give you the exact match and better results.

Google Gravity:

If, by any chance, you feeling like searching “Google gravity” and hitting “I’m feeling lucky”, don’t be surprised if Google comes crashing down around you the second you move the mouse. (Hint: you can disable Google’s instant results in your account preferences page to make sure you get the ‘I’m feeling lucky’ option).Also search for Google space

Play Atari Breakout:

Most of us know Atari Breakout is a very old and popular game. If you ever got bored in Internet and want to play a game, you can play Atari Breakout in Google images by searching Atari Breakout.But first you must wait for the page to load it completely.

Find Holidays:

Quickly find the details of holidays by searching “Festival name and Year”.

Blink HTML:

Google Blink HTML and see how the HTML blink in the page.

Fun Facts:

Do you want to know some fun facts? If yes, Google can entertain you with random fun facts without leaving Google homepage. Just search for “Fun facts” or “i’m feeling curious”.

Find my phone:

Google has launched Android device manager to find lost android devices. But now Google search can also this. Just search for “Find my phone” to locate your Android device remotely. But you need to login your Gmail account to locate it.
